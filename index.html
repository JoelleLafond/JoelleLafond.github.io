I've always been fascinated by the weird, surprising phenomena that can be discovered when studying biology. Working on atypical reproductions was then only natural for me!

I have recently closed a big chapter of my life, as my PhD thesis has been accepted in May, 2024, and been chosen as candidate for both the Rector's Honor List & Best Thesis Awards. As of January 2024, I started a postdoc at the University of British Columbia (UBC), in the lab of the awesome Dr. Judith Mank. I am now working on the evolution of sex chromosomes in the awesome guppies! Stay tuned for more exciting research!
